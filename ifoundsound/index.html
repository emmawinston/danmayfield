iFOUNDSOUND design sound installations and workshops which explore ways in which children and adults can discover listening, capturing and exploring sound.

As citizens we are the composers, performers, conductors and audiences of our acoustic environment. We hope the workshops open the minds and ears of participants to a greater appreciation of sound and its significance to our own and others' wellbeing.
