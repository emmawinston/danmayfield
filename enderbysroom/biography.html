Biography

Enderby's Room are a London-based indie-folk band specialising in lush harmonies, unusual instruments, and vivid storytelling. Songwriter Dan Mayfield has previously performed with Darren Hayman, Daniel Johnston, and the Wave Pictures; with Enderby's Room, he now performs his own music backed by harmonium, double bass and ukuleles.

At times they capture the sumptuous melancholia of Low; at others, they're finding new shapes for indie folk. --How Does It Feel To Be Loved Intricately woven and desperately beautiful music where the lyrics take centre stage. --Folkroom Records
